Instructions

Tell our AI what to create and the desired style.

Be specific. For instance, "Bob smoking a purple cigarette" is better than "Bob smoking".

For custom styles, end descriptions with "in the style of X" where X is the custom style. See examples.

Custom Examples:

  • Happy raindrops in the style of Vincent Van Gogh's painting, Starry Night
  • DNA molecule in the style of the Unreal Engine
  • Mountains in the style of Huang Gongwang
  • Snoopy as a hipster in the style of Picasso

256x256 images take about 3 minutes while 512x512 images take about 7 minutes.

To modify seed images, provide specific and realistic directions. Our AI is good, but not God. Enter text to guide our AI into making reasonable changes to the seed image.

Seed Image Examples:

  • Bad: Make our founder as handsome as Brad Pitt
  • Good: Make this dragon red
  • Bad: Make our founder look less awkward in this prom photo
  • Good: Add a purple sunset to this beach painting

Art Maker FAQ

  • Can I sell graphics or use them for NFTs?

    Yes, but first purchase credits: one license per graphic.

    Second, treat Hotpot as a digital helper who augments your workflow, someone to help brainstorm and automate tedious work, but not something to replace the creative process. We cannot guarantee uniquess anymore than Photoshop can. It is possible for someone to produce something comparable with other AI or software, much like any physical painting can be reproduced.

    Ultimately, it's the user's responsibility to apply unique modifications and to ensure intellectual property compliance.

  • Can you guarantee someone else won't create the same graphic?

    No service can guarantee this.

    Even if we guarantee that no one uses your identical prompt, others may use similar prompts that produce similar graphics. For example, if another graphic looks 95% the same as yours, is yours truly unique? The safest way to treat Hotpot is like a digital helper who augments your workflow, someone to help brainstorm and automate tedious work, but not something to replace the creative process.

  • What is creation time?

    Creation time defines how long our AI spends creating your graphic. Typically, more time translates into more details and higher quality, but this depends on the style you're aiming for. Sometimes a less polished, more abstract graphic produces more desirable results.

  • Will AI replace humans?

    If you believe this, we recommend using Siri more often. :)

    On a serious note, we see AI more as "augmented intelligence" -- technology that augments people rather than replaces them. In the long term, it is possible scientists and researchers invent general intelligence that one day displaces humans, but that day is certainly not today.

    Those who deride the limitations of machine learning overlook how many paid human tasks require little intelligence. More concretely, AI could immensely benefit humanity even without achieving AGI.

  • I'm on a budget. Can you offer a discount?

    Yes, contact us. Our pricing philosophy is to help the poor and charge the rich.
