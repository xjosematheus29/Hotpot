Volume Pricing

Our philosophy is to keep prices low enough so you can easily justify the convenience and value of Hotpot. We look forward to seeing how you use Hotpot technology.

Unlike other services, we only charge for characters translated -- format characters are excluded. For example, <div>Hello</div> only qualifies as 5 characters because only the word "hello" is localized. If you translate "hello" into two languages, the service charges for 10 characters.

Glossaries & Custom Formats

We support glossaries to use specific translations for certain words and phrases. Glossaries allow you to define custom translations for words and phrases, overriding the default result.

Other file formats are also supported.

To use glossaries or to translate other formats, please contact us to join the beta program.
